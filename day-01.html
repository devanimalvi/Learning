Almonds

Almonds are one of the most popular dry fruits. Other common dry fruits include cashews, pistachios, raisins, and walnuts.

Health Benefits of Almonds

Almonds are rich in vitamin E, fiber, and healthy fats. Eating almonds daily can improve heart health and brain function.
